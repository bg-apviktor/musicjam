## Brand

## Color Palette
- Primary Raspberry: **#B5174A** — hero bg overlay, buttons, headings
- Accent Green: **#3EA66B** — secondary CTAs, nature accents
- Background Cream: **#FDF8F4** — page bg
- Surface White: **#FFFFFF** — cards
- Surface Alt (pink tint): **#F9ECEF** — alternate sections
- Text Dark Plum: **#2D1A22**
- Text Muted: **#7A5C68**

## Typography
- Primary: **Nunito** (rounded sans-serif, supports Cyrillic)
- Decorative: **Caveat** (handwritten, for labels and accents)
- H1: 56px / bold / -2% tracking
- H2: 40px / semibold / -1% tracking
- H3: 28px / semibold
- Body: 17px / regular / 1.6 line-height
- Caption: 13px

## Layout Tokens
- Max width: 1120px, centered
- Section padding: 88px vertical
- Card radius: 18px
- Button radius: 28px (pill shape)
- Alternate sections: white / surfaceAlt backgrounds

## Navigation
Sticky top nav: transparent → white on scroll
Links: Home | About | History | Merch | Donate | Social
Language switcher: ET | RU | EN (top right)

## Section Design Specs

**hero** — Full-width, 100vh. Warm camp photo bg + raspberry overlay (0.6 opacity). Large white H1, subtitle, pill CTA button. Logo top-left in nav.

**intro_tagline** — Centered, cream bg. Medium H2, body text, small secondary CTA. Decorative Caveat font accent above heading.

**quick_stats** — 3 large numbers side by side: "Since 2018" / "5+ Cities" / "3 Languages". Bold H1-size numbers in raspberry, caption labels below. Surfacealt bg.

**about_intro** — Two-column: text left (H2 + body + CTA), illustration right. Cream bg.

**activities_grid** — 2×3 grid of cards. Each card: icon (flat illustration, brand colors) + bold title + short body. White cards, soft shadow, 18px radius.

**history_heading** — Centered H2 + subtitle. Decorative Caveat label above.

**camp_timeline** — Vertical timeline. 6 nodes: Tapa 2018 → Tapa 2019 → Tapa 2020 → Narva 2021 → Kohila 2022 → Haapsalu 2023. Raspberry dots, alternating left/right cards.

**merch_heading + merch_products** — SurfaceAlt bg. H2 + 3-4 product placeholder cards in a row. Each card: image placeholder + name + "Shop Now" button.

**donate_panel** — Full-width raspberry bg. White H2: "Support MusicJam". White body text. Big white pill CTA "Donate Today" + PayPal button. Trust text below in smaller font.

**donate_trust** — Below donate, cream bg. Small caption about fund usage.

**social_links** — Centered. H2 "Connect With Us". 3 large social icons: Instagram @musicjam_ / YouTube musicjamcamp / Facebook musicjamcamp. Hover states with brand color.

**footer_strip** — Dark plum bg (#2D1A22). Logo + tagline "Play. Worship. Grow." centered. Language switcher. Copyright © MusicJam 2018–2026.

**cookie_banner** — Floating bottom strip, dark bg, white text, "Accept" pill button in accent green.
